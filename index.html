📘 How to Use the International Staff Scheduler

Step 1: Enter staff names, start time, hours worked, and select the days they work. The end time will be calculated automatically.

Step 2: Click Update & Save to generate the Heatmap and Daily Schedules.

Step 3: Use the Show half-hour data toggle to refine time resolution, and select the optional time zones you want displayed beneath Primary US Eastern time. Small +1 or -1 markers indicate a next-day or previous-day conversion.

Step 4: You can Import or Export data as CSV, Clear the staff list, or load Sample Data for demo purposes.

Your entries are saved locally in your browser and will be restored on your next visit.

We've pre-loaded example data on your first visit to help you explore the tool quickly. You can clear or replace it anytime.
